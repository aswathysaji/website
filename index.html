    About

    ROBOCEK is a teaching and learning community established in the Government College of Engineering, Kannur. Originally founded in 2013 by a team of enthusiasts, inspired from a group of students from Chennai, it attained its official recognition on 30th January, 2014.

    ROBOCEK is a dedicated cluster of energetic enthusiasts and hobbyists, that strives to impart passion for innovation and inclusion. We are committed to academic excellence, student success and community engagement. We organise sessions, workshops, competitions and expeditions nurturing innovation and learning, endeavouring to keep in pace with the ever evolving field of electronics and robotics.

    Mission

    ROBOCEK aspires to foster young minds to new ingisights of the world of robotics. The club creates a climate full of energy for the creative involvement of begineers and enthusiasts among the student community. The club facilitates a diverse learning environment aiding in design, building, operation and control of robotic systems through hands-on projects and throught collaboration with experienced advisors and mentors

    Vision

    ROBOCEK endeavours to create a community of strong responsible engineers who can intellectually face the needs and challenges of tomorrow and also respect universal values

    Values

    ROBOCEK strives to excel as a learning community by adapting, learning and continually improving our team. This is done by encouraging initiative and involvement thereby fostering healthy ethical and social relations while at the same time, adhering to sustainable ideas
